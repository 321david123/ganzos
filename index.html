Our Story

Three generations.
One family table.

Ganzo's is a family owned and operated Mexican restaurant in Davenport, Iowa, serving our delicious family recipes to the greater Quad City area since the early 1960s.

Founder John Ynocencio "Ganzo" Puente made one thing clear: when you come to Ganzo's, you will always leave satisfied. Whether you're a local or just passing through, come in and experience three generations of passed-down family recipes — now led by third-generation owner Pat Puente.

"Never go home hungry."

— John Ynocencio "Ganzo" Puente, Founder
